Myelomeningocele

Educational overview (not a substitute for medical advice).

What is Myelomeningocele?

Myelomeningocele is a severe type of spina bifida where the spinal cord and nerves protrude through an opening in the spine.

Typical Signs

  • Visible sac/swelling on baby’s back
  • Leg weakness or reduced movement
  • Bladder/bowel problems
  • Sometimes associated hydrocephalus

Management Overview

  • Early surgical evaluation (timing depends on case)
  • Protection of exposed tissues and infection prevention
  • Long-term follow-up: mobility, bladder care, rehabilitation
