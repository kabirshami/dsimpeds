Spinal Trauma

Educational overview (seek emergency care for severe symptoms).

Overview

Spinal trauma can occur due to falls, accidents or sports injuries. Some injuries require urgent stabilization and imaging.

Emergency Symptoms

  • Neck/back pain with weakness or numbness
  • Difficulty walking
  • Loss of bladder/bowel control
  • Severe neck/back pain after major injury

Evaluation

  • Immobilization and neurological examination
  • X-ray/CT/MRI based on clinical needs
  • Monitoring and specialist management
